Caramelized Pineapple

Description

If you love baked pineapple, then you are going to fall head over heels for this oven caramelized baked Brazilian pineapple, also called Brazilian grilled pineapple.

This amazing baked pineapple recipe can be made in the oven or grill. The flavor is absolute perfection! Sweet and lightly spiced cinnamon baked pineapple makes a delicious side dish for juicy, bacon-wrapped pork or dessert treat with this orange bundt cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 large pineapple peeled, cored and cut into spears
  • 1 cup brown sugar packed
  • 1-3 teaspoons cinnamon ground
  • 2-4 tablespoons butter COLD, cubed
  • cooking spray

Instructions

  1. Get out and measure your ingredients.
  2. Cut your pineapple into spears.
  3. Combine brown sugar and cinnamon in a large resealable bag.
  4. Add the pineapple spears and close the bag tightly.
  5. Shake it so that the cinnamon and brown sugar completely coat the spears. Press sugar mixture onto spears as you remove from bag.
  6. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Prepare a rimmed baking sheet with a tin foil and a light coating of cooking spray. Set aside.
  7. Place coated pineapple on prepared baking sheet in a single layer. Place tiny butter cubes across the top of the pineapple spears.
  8. Place prepared pineapple spears directly on top of the grill, with or without a skewer stuck inside it lengthwise. Just like a big pineapple popsicle or lollipop.
  9. Cook covered until soft and lightly charred; 2-3 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of your pineapple.
